Privacy Policy

Natural Sanitation LLC · Last updated April 14, 2026

This policy describes how Natural Sanitation LLC (“we,” “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website, request service information, or purchase a subscription.

Information we collect

  • Contact and account details: name, phone number, email, service address, and ZIP code when you submit forms or complete checkout.
  • Payment data: card payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your full card number. Stripe may share limited billing status with us (for example, last four digits and charge confirmations).
  • Messages and photos: before-and-after photos and SMS or email communications related to your service.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and similar analytics data when you use our site.

How we use information

  • To schedule service, route crews, and send service updates (including SMS with your consent).
  • To process payments and send receipts.
  • To improve our website and advertising, including measuring ad performance.
  • To comply with law and protect our rights.

Sharing

We share information with service providers that help us operate the business, including Stripe (payments), Jobber (scheduling and billing workflows), hosting providers, and analytics or advertising platforms you encounter on our site. We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

You may opt out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe link. SMS programs include instructions to reply STOP to opt out. You may contact us to access, correct, or delete certain personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.
